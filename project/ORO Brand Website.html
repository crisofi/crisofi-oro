THE STORY

Gilded light.
Timeless ritual.

Inspired by the warm, gilded light of the Mediterranean golden hour. ORO is a concentrated milk bath designed for deep restoration.

Pure, creamy goat's milk — a timeless beauty ritual — melts into the water to create a silken, skin-softening veil. Each crystal carries the warmth of aged Cedar, the brightness of Bergamot, and the gentle bloom of Jasmine.

Refined, intentional, and evocative of a private villa retreat.
